About

Novus is a 501(c)(3) not-for-profit educational organization launched just over a year ago. Novus Club has humble beginnings as it was founded by three high school students who saw a problem with their high school careers.

Within high schools there are student business organizations and technology organizations, however there are none that combine the two interests.

In a society that is becoming increasingly reliant on technology, it is vital to promote the cross-sections of computer science and business and that is what Novus strives to do
